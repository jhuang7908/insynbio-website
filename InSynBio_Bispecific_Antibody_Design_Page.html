AI-Native Design Platform

Our platform integrates clinical data with structural AI to de-risk multispecific development. We don't just model; we benchmark.

  • Clinical Knowledge Base (125+) — Comprehensive analysis of 125+ clinical-stage bispecifics to guide target pairing and format selection.
  • Structural Benchmark (84+) — A proprietary dataset of 84 validated linker geometries (scFv & IgG-scFv fusions) to prevent conformational collapse.
  • CMC Benchmark (75+) — Deep structural metrics from 75 IgG-like architectures (50 Four-Arm + 23 Common-LC + 2 VHH-IgG) to predict mispairing risks.

Nano-Convert™ (VH to VHH)

Unlock the power of Nanobodies from your existing antibody assets. No llama immunization required.

Feature Description
Camelization Introduction of VHH hallmark mutations (FR2) to solubility VH domains without light chains.
Tier-Based Humanization Proprietary Tier 0-3 system to balance immunogenicity and affinity. Preserves critical Vernier Zone residues.
Affinity Recovery Structure-guided CDR back-mutations to restore binding lost during scaffold transfer.

SmartLink™ Optimization

Data-driven linker selection, not guesswork. We screen your design against our library of clinical linkers.

  • Geometry Matching — Selects linker length (G4S, EAAAK, etc.) based on the precise distance between epitopes.
  • Co-folding Validation — AlphaFold2 Multimer modeling of the ternary complex (Ab + Ag1 + Ag2) to verify simultaneous binding.
  • Flexibility Scoring — Balances entropic penalty (too flexible) vs. steric hindrance (too rigid).

Structural Audit

Benchmark your design against clinical-stage therapeutics. Know your risk profile before you clone.

Deliverable: The Scoring Card

  • Stability Score (pLDDT, PAE)
  • Interface Quality (BSA, Sc, H-bonds)
  • Domain Orientation (VH-VL angles vs. clinical distribution)
  • Risk Ranking (Top 10% / Median / Bottom 10%)

Virtual CMC

Solve chain mispairing before wet-lab production. Essential for IgG-like formats (CrossMab, KiH).

  • Mispairing Prediction — Energy gap calculation between correct (H1L1) and incorrect (H1L2) pairs.
  • Interface Engineering — Design of charge repulsion (electrostatic steering) or steric complementarity mutations to enforce correct pairing.
  • Developability Check — Full-spectrum assessment of aggregation (SAP), viscosity, and chemical stability.

Full Design Report

We deliver a comprehensive design package ready for synthesis.

  • Optimized Sequences — DNA/AA sequences for all chains/domains.
  • Structural Models — PDB files of the predicted bispecific molecule (and complex).
  • Assessment Data — Detailed scoring cards and risk analysis.
  • Rationale — Scientific justification for linker selection and mutations.
