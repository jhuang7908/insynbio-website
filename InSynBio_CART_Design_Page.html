  • Antigen Knowledge Base ‚Äî A continuously updated database of common epitopes, antigen density thresholds, heterogeneity profiles, disease context (solid vs. hematologic vs. autoimmune), and specificity considerations (e.g. on-target off-tumor risk) to guide precise design.
  • Smart Vector Logic ‚Äî Algorithmic selection between Lentiviral (Standard), Non-Viral/Transposon (Large Payload), and In Vivo LNP (Accessibility) based on clinical needs.

Therapeutic Architecture Matrix

Our AI engine applies deep biological logic to select the optimal architecture. We don't just assemble parts; we design systems to overcome specific disease barriers.

Disease Context Critical Barriers Advanced Architectural Solution
Solid Tumors (e.g., HCC, Gastric) Physical Exclusion (ECM), Immunosuppression (TGF-Œ≤) & On-Target Off-Tumor Matrix-Degrading Synergy: CAR-M to degrade ECM + CAR-T armored with TGFB_DNR. Affinity Tuning (lower Kd on normal-tissue antigens) and Logic-Gated recognition to spare healthy tissue.
Hematologic / Liquid Tumors (e.g., B-ALL, LBCL) Cytokine Storm (CRS) & B-cell Aplasia 4-1BB-Dominant (reduced CRS vs CD28), Safety Switches (tEGFR, iCasp9) for controllable elimination, Tuned Affinity and dose to balance efficacy and toxicity.

CNS / Glioblastoma Blood-Brain Barrier & Neurotoxicity (ICANS) Regional Precision: Intracavitary Delivery (ICV) bypasses BBB. Hinge/TM Optimization minimizes tonic signaling to reduce ICANS risk.

  • Vector & Capacity ‚Äî Compact VHH designs to save payload space; PiggyBac / Sleeping Beauty Transposon support for ultra-large constructs (>10kb) surpassing lentiviral limits.
