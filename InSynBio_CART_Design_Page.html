AI-Native Design PlatformAI 原生设计平台

Our ACTES platform leverages a proprietary library of hundreds of curated components across 10 categories. We don't just assemble; we match.

ACTES 平台基于包含 10 大类、数百条精选元件的专有库。我们不仅仅是组装，而是精准匹配。

  • Antigen Knowledge Base — A continuously updated database of common epitopes, antigen density thresholds, and heterogeneity profiles to guide precise design.
  • Smart Vector Logic — Algorithmic selection between Lentiviral (Standard), Non-Viral/Transposon (Large Payload), and In Vivo LNP (Accessibility) based on clinical needs.
  • Epitope-Hinge Matching — We measure epitope-to-membrane distance using AlphaFold2 to select the precise Hinge length (Short/Medium/Long).
  • Density-Signaling Logic — We analyze antigen density to select the optimal Transmembrane (CD8α vs CD28) and Signaling domains (4-1BB vs CD28).
  • 抗原知识库 — 维护持续更新的数据库，包含常见表位、抗原密度阈值及异质性图谱，指导精准设计。
  • 智能载体逻辑 — 根据临床需求算法化选择：慢病毒 (标准)、非病毒/转座子 (大载荷) 或 体内 LNP (可及性)。
  • 表位-铰链匹配 — 利用 AlphaFold2 测量表位到细胞膜的距离，精准选择铰链长度（短/中/长）。
  • 密度-信号逻辑 — 分析抗原密度，选择最优的跨膜域（CD8α vs CD28）与信号域（4-1BB vs CD28）。

Engineering Modules工程化模块

We assemble the optimal CAR construct using a validated library of functional domains.

我们使用经过验证的功能域库组装最优 CAR 构建体。

  • Binders — scFv (Clinical standard), VHH (Solid tumor penetration), TCR-mimic (Intracellular targets via pMHC).
  • Hinge & TM — Auto-Hinge selection (Short/Medium/Long) matching epitope distance; CD8α TM (Low tonic signaling) vs CD28 TM (Lipid raft clustering).
  • Signaling — 4-1BB (Persistence/Memory) vs CD28 (Rapid activation/Effector).
  • Armoring (Payloads) — TGFB_DNR (Anti-suppression), Membrane_IL15 (Persistence), GPX4 (Anti-ferroptosis), Secreted IL-12 (TRUCK).
  • Vector & Capacity — Compact VHH designs to save payload space; PiggyBac Transposon support for ultra-large constructs (>10kb) surpassing lentiviral limits.
  • 结合域 (Binders) — scFv (临床标准), VHH (实体瘤穿透), TCR-mimic (通过 pMHC 识别胞内靶点)。
  • 铰链与跨膜 (Hinge & TM) — Auto-Hinge 选型（短/中/长）匹配表位距离；CD8α TM (低基底信号) vs CD28 TM (脂筏聚集)。
  • 信号域 (Signaling) — 4-1BB (持久性/记忆) vs CD28 (快速激活/效应)。
  • 铠甲 (Armoring) — TGFB_DNR (抗抑制), Membrane_IL15 (持久性), GPX4 (抗铁死亡), 分泌型 IL-12 (TRUCK)。
  • 载体与容量 (Vector & Capacity) — 紧凑型 VHH 设计节省载荷空间；支持 PiggyBac 转座子 系统，突破慢病毒限制，承载超大构建体 (>10kb)。

Safety & Efficacy安全与有效性

Safety is engineered into the sequence, not just monitored in the clinic.

安全性是设计进序列中的，而不仅仅是在临床中监测。

  • Safety Switches — iCasp9 (Standard, >95% clearance), tEGFR (ADCC), RQR8 (CD20 mimotope).
  • Logic Gating — SynNotch (AND gate for specificity), iCAR (Inhibitory CAR for normal tissue protection).
  • Toxicity Management — 4-1BB to reduce CRS peak; Local delivery to minimize systemic toxicity.
  • 安全开关 — iCasp9 (标准, >95% 清除), tEGFR (ADCC), RQR8 (CD20 拟态表位)。
  • 逻辑门控 — SynNotch (AND 门提升特异性), iCAR (抑制型 CAR 保护正常组织)。
  • 毒性管理 — 使用 4-1BB 降低 CRS 峰值；局部递送减少全身毒性。
