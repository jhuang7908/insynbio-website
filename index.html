AI for Drug R&DAI 驱动药物研发

InSynBio focuses on AI-assisted drug R&D. We leverage clinical and scientific literature data, combined with machine learning, to deliver AI-driven drug design and evaluation. Our approach integrates computational and experimental workflows (dry-wet combined), supporting clinical translation and drug innovation.
